Capabilities

Everything you need to assess risk

From evidence ingestion to a layman-readable PDF report — one authenticated workspace.

🛡️
Consent-First Design
Every assessment requires explicit scope authorization. Consent receipts are hash-chained for provenance and auditability.
🗺️
MITRE ATT&CK Mapping
Findings are automatically linked to CWE and MITRE ATT&CK techniques via a configurable YAML rule engine.
📊
Risk Scoring
Priority-ranked P0–P3 scores using CVSS, EPSS, KEV signals and reachability context, with full explanations.
📋
Control Domain Analysis
16+ NIST SP 800-53 and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 domains enriched with actionable remediation steps for every failed control.
✅
Legitimacy Verification
Automated cross-checks that verify totals, risk scores, evidence counts, and recommendations are mutually consistent.
🤖
AI Assistant
Ask "What should I do next?" and receive plain-language guidance with safe, suggested next actions you can action immediately.
